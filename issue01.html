Welcome to Brazilian Wax Broadcast 💌

You might know us from at your Service, the May Day Rave, or way back from Coven. Now we’re opening up this space to stay closer in touch with you — beyond the noise of the algorithm.

We’re starting this newsletter to bring everything together in one place — and to slowly step away from relying on Instagram (you probably get why).

What to expect:

💃 Party & event updates

🎧 Fresh mixes and new music

📚 Readings, reflections & queer perspectives

📍 Our agenda — and events we recommend

✨ A space to celebrate house music’s roots in dissident, queer communities

House music was born from the margins. It’s time we bring it back — queerer, freer, and louder than ever.

If you like what we’re doing, share it with your friends.

Got ideas, feedback, or love notes? Hit reply and talk to us.
